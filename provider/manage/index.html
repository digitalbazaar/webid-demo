About this page

This page is a technology demonstration of WebID implemented in Javascript and Flash. The WebIDs created on this page can be used to seamlessly log into demonstration WebID-enabled websites. If you would like to learn more about this technology demo, WebID and Universal Login for the Web is a good place to start.

Why is WebID Awesome?

WebID is a replacement for traditional login on the Web. It is an open standard for identity and login. With WebID you no longer need to remember usernames or passwords for all of the sites you use. You can publish your identity wherever you want and choose what pieces of your personal information that you want to share with websites you sign up with. With WebID, creating and account and logging into a website is as simple as just selecting your existing WebID.

Troubleshooting

Since this is an alpha-quality technology demonstration, it may not work in all browsers. Specifically, Internet Explorer 6 and 7 have not been tested yet. We suggest that you run this demo in Google Chrome, although it has also been tested successfully in Firefox 3.5+ and Safari 4+.
